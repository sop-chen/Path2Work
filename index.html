Path2Work

Path2Work supports refugees in Switzerland with job searching. You can be recommended occupations and search for specific jobs here.

Path2Work can be accessed in 9 languages:

  • English
  • German
  • French
  • Italian
  • Turkish
  • Arabic
  • Farsi
  • Ukrainian
  • Tigrinya
